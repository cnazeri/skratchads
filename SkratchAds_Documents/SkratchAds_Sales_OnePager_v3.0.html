The Solution

How SkratchAds Works

5 simple steps from impression to qualified lead.

1

Ad Appears

Banner-sized gamified ad invites users to scratch for a prize.

2

User Scratches

3–5 second voluntary gesture. No forced interaction. Pure choice.

3

Win Path

Real prize revealed (gift cards, discounts). User is primed and interested.

4

Lose Path

Near-miss effect shows primed brand message. Highest-attention ad moment.

5

Redeem

Email entry to claim prize. Qualified, self-selected lead captured.
