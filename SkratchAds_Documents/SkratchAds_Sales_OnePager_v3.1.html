The Mechanics

How SkratchAds™ Works

A voluntary, interactive scratch-off that converts because users choose to engage.

1

Ad Appears

A golden scratch-off banner appears in feed or banner slot. The user sees "Scratch to Win" and a notification dot. Curiosity triggers engagement.

2

User Scratches

The user drags their finger across the banner. Gray scratch residue blobs reveal content underneath. The tactile action triggers dopamine and keeps them engaged.

3

Win Path

If they win, a celebration screen erupts: golden sunburst, trophy emoji, "YOU WON!" text, confetti dots, and a "Claim Now" button. Emotional peak drives conversion.

4

Lose Path

No win? A premium dark screen with star field shows "Better Luck Next Time" and a faded LOSE watermark. The brand message appears in a subtle pill. Still engaging, never intrusive.

5

Redeem

Winners land on the redemption screen. Trophy, "Prize Unlocked", warm golden background, email input, "Redeem Now" button. Email capture. Campaign complete.
