Our Story

Building the Ad Format
the Industry Actually Needs

SkratchAds was born from a simple observation: banner ads are broken for everyone, and nobody has built a better replacement that works simultaneously for publishers, advertisers, and users.

Mission

Why We Exist

"To replace banner blindness with genuine engagement — by giving users something worth scratching for, publishers revenue worth earning, and advertisers attention worth paying for."

The scratch-off lottery mechanic is one of the most reliable engagement triggers in human psychology. Americans spend over $100 billion annually on scratch-off tickets — voluntarily, for any chance at a reward. The behavior is deeply ingrained.

We asked a simple question: what if mobile advertising worked like that? What if, instead of a banner nobody looks at, users saw an invitation to scratch — and were rewarded for engaging?

The result is SkratchAds: the first voluntary scratch-off mobile ad format with a dual conversion path. Win path users get a real prize and the advertiser gets a qualified email lead. Lose path users see a brand ad after 3–5 seconds of active engagement — delivering 5–10× the CTR of a cold banner impression.

What We Believe

Our Values

Voluntary First

We will never force engagement. Every interaction with SkratchAds is a user choice. If users don't want to scratch, they don't have to. That's not a bug — it's the point.

Real Value, Real Prizes

Virtual coins and badge rewards are not real incentives. We offer actual prizes — because real value is the only thing that drives authentic voluntary engagement.

Publisher First

Publishers take the most risk building audiences. They should earn the most. 70% revenue share is our commitment — not a promotional rate.

No Dark Patterns

No fake urgency, no manipulative mechanics, no misleading prize odds. Every aspect of SkratchAds is designed to be honest with users, publishers, and advertisers.

Privacy by Design

Built for the post-ATT world. No behavioral tracking. No third-party cookies. Engagement-based, consent-driven, and compliant by architecture — not by workaround.

Long Game

We are building a lasting format, not a growth hack. The original idea was conceived in 2011. We've spent over a decade making sure we get it right.

History

The Long Road to SkratchAds

This is a second iteration, not a first attempt. The lessons of the past are built into every decision.

2011

The Original Idea

Concept first originated — a scratch-off mechanic for mobile advertising. The mobile ad industry was too early for the format, but the idea was filed away.

2011–2014

First Iteration

First version built and launched. Generated valuable lessons about publisher adoption, prize mechanics, and ad network integration. Sunset as the team learned what the second version needed to be.

2024

Revival & Rebuild

Market conditions finally caught up with the vision. Apple ATT ended behavioral targeting. Banner blindness reached crisis levels. The second iteration begins — built on a decade of learning.

2025

MVP Ready

Working product with live JS banner integration, server-side win logic, self-serve dashboard, and AWS infrastructure. Free POC program launches for early publisher partners.

2026

Phase 1 Launch

Targeting 50–200 app integrations. First brand advertiser campaigns. Native iOS and Android SDK development begins. Seed funding round.

2027+

Scale

Native SDK releases. Mediation adapter integrations (ironSource, MAX). Internal RTB auction engine. 500–20,000+ app integrations. $1M–$100M ARR trajectory.
