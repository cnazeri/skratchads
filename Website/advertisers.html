How It Works for Advertisers

Two Ways to Win on Every Scratch

Every user who scratches your SkratchAd enters one of two conversion paths — both deliver value. No impression is wasted.

Win Path

Qualified Lead with Email Capture

When a user wins, they see a prize overlay and voluntarily submit their email to claim it. You receive a consent-based, intent-qualified lead — not a passive impression.

  • User scratches and wins a real prize
  • Prize overlay reveals your brand + offer
  • User enters email to claim reward
  • You receive opted-in email lead
  • Token issued — user redeems prize
Lose Path

Primed Brand Impression

When a user doesn't win, your brand ad reveals to a user who just spent 3–5 seconds actively engaging. Attention is primed — CTR is 5–10× higher than a cold banner impression.

  • User scratches — no prize this time
  • Your brand ad reveals in the cleared space
  • User is engaged, curious, and attentive
  • 5–10× CTR uplift vs. standard banner
  • User clicks through to your landing page

Why SkratchAds

Built for the Post-ATT World

Apple's ATT framework and cookie deprecation ended behavioral targeting. Engagement is now the primary value signal — and SkratchAds is the only format built around voluntary, rewarded engagement.

Consent-Based Leads

Every email captured on the win path is 100% opt-in. Users submit their email voluntarily to claim their prize — no dark patterns, no scraped data.

No Wasted Impressions

You pay per scratch, not per thousand impressions. Every dollar goes toward a user who actively chose to engage with your brand.

Primed Attention

The lose path delivers your brand to a user who just spent 3–5 seconds in active engagement. CTR uplift of 5–10× vs. cold banner impressions.

Real Prize Association

Your brand is associated with giving users something valuable — a real prize. This builds positive brand sentiment that a banner impression never could.

Privacy Compliant
