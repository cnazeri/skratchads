## EMAIL SENDING RULES

1. **Personalize every Email 1** — mention their specific app or company, never send a generic blast
2. **Send Tuesday–Thursday, 9am–11am** — highest open rates
3. **Max 3 emails per lead** — Email 1, Follow-Up 1, Follow-Up 2. Never more.
4. **Always respond within 2 hours** to any reply — speed signals professionalism
5. **Keep emails short** — under 150 words for follow-ups, under 250 for Email 1
6. **One CTA per email** — don't ask for a call AND more info AND a form fill. Pick one.
7. **Plain text only** — no HTML templates, no fancy formatting. Plain text emails get higher open rates and feel more personal.
